    Our Additional Services

    As a reputed company we are fulfilled with lot of resourses. As a result of this these resourses are used to provide these services to other companies as well.

    CRUSHER PLANTS

    NE BUILDERS has made strategic investments in three crusher plants that manufacture crushed rock aggregates, which are used as a raw material in the production of asphalt, concrete, and dense grade aggregates. Our project portfolio necessitates large quantities of aggregate-based materials, and by running our own aggregate processing plants, we can ensure a more consistent supply and the long-term viability of our construction operations. In addition, we have ensured that all quarry by-products, including quarry dust, are used in some way in the construction process. The average total daily production of these plants is around 150 Cubes.

    SUPPLY OF ASPHALT CONCRETE

    One of the most important materials used in the building of roads and highways is asphalt. The demand for Asphalt Concrete increased rapidly in tandem with the rapid increase in infrastructure projects in the country, the majority of which are roads and highways. These plants have a combined capacity of about 4,200 metric tons of asphalt concrete. These asphalt mixing plants, which are located throughout the world, serve both internal and external needs.

    READY-MIX CONCRETE SUPPLY

    NE BUILDERS owns and operates two ready-mix concrete facilities, each with a capacity of 60m3/hr and 20m3/hr. Small-scale plants are often installed at project-specific locations. Although these plants were initially built to meet the needs of internal projects, the nature of the ready mix concrete and the consistency of our supply have helped us secure a number of external clients. A fleet of modern machinery and equipment, including a 60m3/hr Twin Shaft concrete batching facility, a 5,000 l/hr water chiller plant, a brand new 36m Boom pump car, and a 17m Boom pump car, supports the service provided by the larger plant, which is located on the outskirts of Colombo City. Protected aggregate containers, cement storage silos, and computerized weight.
